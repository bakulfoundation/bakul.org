Sujit Mahapatra, the Festival Director of BhuFeSto is the person behind the very popular jingle, "Rabibaar Gapabaar". The Founder of Bakul Foundation, Sujit Mahapatra calls himself a "reluctant storyteller" and has preferred being backstage and organizing storytelling and storytelling festivals. The curator of the popular series, Rabibaar Gapabaar and Storytime @ 9 on the Bakul Foundation youtube channel, Sujit will be telling stories at BhuFeSto 2024.

Santakar Chelapila , a seasoned educator and Senior Reporter at ETV Bharat Odia, seamlessly combines teaching, journalism, and storytelling. Saswati Pattnaik, a certified storyteller is passionate about storytelling and culinary arts. The many feathers in her hat include storyteller, trainer, anchor, and food blogger. Sujata Priyamba, a distinguished theatre artist with 25 years in acting and 10 in directing, has received specialized training from global luminaries.
