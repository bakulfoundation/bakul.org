The FLYBRARY at Bhubaneswar Airport is an initiative of Bakul Foundation in partnership with the Airports Authority of India. Bakul Foundation has been running a library movement in Odisha entirely with the spirit of volunteerism to promote the habit of reading among people and children in particular. As part of that movement, Bakul has been setting up libraries in public spaces like airports, bus stations, parks, museums and cafes.


There are 2 shelves, one each at DEPARTURE and at ARRIVALS. You can pick up a book of your choice, read and return it at the shelf there. Or you can also take the book with yourself.


You can return the books at the Flybrary Shelves at the Bhubaneswar airport or send them by post to Bakul Foundation. You can also gift your books to Bakul for the Flybrary initiative or the Library Movement in general for the libraries Bakul is setting up across Odisha. In fact, the books at the Flybrary have been donated by readers to Bakul Foundation.
