  • Paint The City

  • Bakul Foundation is an attempt to demonstrate the power of volunteerism. Ever since its inception in 2007, Bakul has played a major role in using Public Art to create awareness and to beautify the city. Bakul has organized several public art events in which citizens including children, youth, artists (both eminent and amateur) and others have participated.


    Bakul Foundation has partnered with the Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation in the Street Art & Murals Project of BMC in painting the flyovers of the city including 3D Street Art by eminent international artists.


  • On 2nd October, 2019 Bakul and BMC are organizing a Public Art event mobilizing citizens of Bhubaneswar to participate in painting a public wall to celebrate 150 years of the birth of Gandhiji and 10 years to Daan Utsav or the Joy of Giving Week, a national festival of giving.

  • Who can participate?

    Anyone can participate. Boys and girls, children & adults, young & old, students & teachers, professionals & amateurs, abled & specially abled. We would particularly want the participation to reflect the diversity of the citizens and include homemakers, senior citizens, social workers, engineers, doctors, civil servants, politicians, corporate professionals, etc.
